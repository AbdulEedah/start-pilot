# Design

## Theme
Dark mode as the primary experience to evoke a "command center" feel. Tinted neutrals (Slate-tinted OKLCH) for a sophisticated depth.

## Colors
- **Neutral**: Tinted Slate (OKLCH: L 0.2, C 0.01, H 250 for dark surfaces).
- **Accent**: Electric Violet (OKLCH: L 0.6, C 0.2, H 300).
- **Success**: Vibrant Emerald (OKLCH: L 0.7, C 0.15, H 150).
- **Warning**: Amber Glow (OKLCH: L 0.8, C 0.1, H 80).
- **Error**: Soft Rose (OKLCH: L 0.6, C 0.15, H 20).

## Typography
- **Headings**: Outfit (Geometric, modern, bold weights).
- **Body**: Inter (High legibility, tabular numbers for data).
- **Scale**: Minor Third (1.20) for a precise, organized feel.

## Spacing & Rhythm
- **Base Unit**: 4px.
- **Radii**: 12px for cards and primary buttons (soft but intentional).
- **Elevation**: Subtle shadows with tinted glows for active states.

## Components
- **AI Bubble**: Glassmorphism with a subtle grain texture and edge glow.
- **Action Cards**: Minimalist with 1px borders (tinted neutrals) and soft hover transitions.
- **Progress Stepper**: Thin, high-precision lines with glowing nodes.
