# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Young entrepreneurs and early-stage founders with raw startup ideas. They are often tech-savvy, ambitious, and looking for clarity in the chaotic early stages of building a business.

## Product Purpose
A full-stack AI-powered startup guidance platform that guides founders through the journey of ideation, validation, tool selection, and roadmap generation. It aims to bridge the gap between a "raw idea" and a "ready-to-pitch" startup.

## Brand Personality
Visionary, Precision, Supportive. The interface should feel like a high-end command center that simplifies complex decisions.

## Anti-references
- Generic corporate SaaS templates.
- Cluttered, information-heavy dashboards.
- "AI Slop" (over-the-top neon gradients or glassmorphism without functional purpose).

## Design Principles
- **Clarity through Depth**: Use elevation and layering to guide focus, not just flat borders.
- **Empathetic Automation**: The AI should feel like a partner, not just a chatbot. Interactions should feel fluid and conversational.
- **Startup Velocity**: Minimize friction in the onboarding and roadmap generation flows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliance.
- Support for high-contrast modes and screen readers.
- Consideration for cognitive load by avoiding unnecessary distractions.
